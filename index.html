About Us

Who we are

Phuty Phuty Drop Inn Centre NPC is a registered non-profit organisation based in Extension 40, Polokwane. We care for nearly 200 children between the ages of 3 and 17, providing them with nutritious food, clothing, cleaning materials, and educational support.

Our doors are open to every child in need — whether they need homework help, a hot meal, or simply a place where they belong.

Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

🌱

Our Vision

To build a healthy, educated community in Polokwane — empowered, well-nourished, and equipped to produce the leaders of tomorrow through mentorship, education, and access to nutritious food and safe shelter.

🤝

Our Mission

To empower communities through essential services — including counselling, nutritional support, rehabilitation referrals, and youth development programs — ensuring every individual lives with dignity and opportunity.

What We Offer

Our Services

From early childhood care to life-skills training, everything we do is designed to meet children and families where they are — and help them move forward.

🍼

Infant & Toddler Care

Safe and nurturing environments for babies and young children — feeding, hygiene, and early learning activities included.

📚

Preschool Education

Structured learning focused on language, mathematics, and social skills to prepare every child for primary school.

🖊️

Aftercare & Homework Support

Homework assistance, guided study sessions, and recreational activities for school-age children after hours.

🥗

Meals & Nutrition

Healthy, balanced meals and snacks every day — because no child can learn or grow on an empty stomach.

💬

Psychosocial Support

Counselling and emotional support services to help children and families cope with trauma, stress, and life challenges.

🧠

Life Skills Development

Activities that teach essential life skills — financial literacy, communication, problem-solving, and more.

🧺

Basic Needs Support

Provision of food, clothing, hygiene assistance, and laundry facilities so every child's basic needs are met.

🌍

Community Engagement

Outreach programs that involve community members in planning and decision-making — building belonging and collaboration.
